How to Report a Potential Vulnerability?
========================================

For public bug reports or feature requests, please
 open an [issue](https://github.com/qualcomm/hexagon-mlir/issues) in the `hexagon-mlir`
 GitHub repository. We will try to address it as soon as possible.

If you prefer to contact us directly, you may also reach us via email at
[hexagon-mlir.support@qti.qualcomm.com](mailto:hexagon-mlir.support@qti.qualcomm.com).
